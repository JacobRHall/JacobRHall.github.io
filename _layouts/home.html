I am currently a postdoctoral fellow in economics at the University of Pennsylvania. I received my PhD in economics from George Mason University in 2023.

I am an applied microeconomist working in the areas of economic history and political economy. My research interests include:

  • The political economy of the premodern state.
  • Economic activity over the long run.
  • Applied Econometrics with a particular emphasis on spatial and network data.
