I am an Assistant Professor in the Salmon P. Chase Center for Civics, Culture, and Society at The Ohio State University.

My research touches on questions in Political Economy, Law & Economics, and Economic Growth by drawing on European and American history. I also make abundant use of spatial and network data.

I received my PhD in Economics from George Mason University in 2023. Prior to joining Ohio State, I was a postdoctoral fellow in the economics department at the University of Pennsylvania and the Penn Initiative for the Study of Markets.
